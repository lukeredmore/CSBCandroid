Tops

  • Girls: Seton logo blouse or white, black, or green turtleneck
  • Boys: White button down shirt with a tie of choice (must be fully tucked in at all times)
  • Seton polos in green, black, or white may be worn in the fall and spring at the discretion of the administration (must be fully tucked in at all times)
  • Green or black Seton vests, fleeces or sweaters jackets may be worn over appropriate dress code
  • During the winter, at the discretion of the administration, any SCC outwear is allowed when worn over appropriate dress code

Bottoms

  • Dress black or khaki pants with a belt
  • Girls may wear the plaid uniform skirt
  • Shorts, leggings, yoga pants, and tight pants/shorts are not acceptable

Footwear

  • Dress shoes (not sneakers) must be worn every day
  • White, black, or green socks, tights, or light colored stockings must be worn
  • Socks or tights in any other color are not acceptable

Physical Education

  • Students change for PE in the locker rooms
  • Sweatpants or shorts, T-shirts and/or sweatshirts
  • Clothing must fit properly and be in good taste

The final decision of acceptability on dress and personal appearance is reserved by the Administration.
