Tops

  • Short or long sleeve polos in green, black, or white
  • Polos solid or with 'S' logo (required on Mass days)
  • Sweaters or sweatshirts may be worn in green, black or white

Bottoms

  • Black or khaki pants/shorts/skorts
  • Jumpers available in green plaid from Lands' End

Footwear

  • Sneakers or other rubber soled shoes
  • Socks must be worn at all times.

Gym Days

  • Gray or green T-shirt/sweatshirt, plain or with the school logo
  • Plain black or grey knee-length, lacrosse type shorts, or plain black or grey sweatpants
  • SAINTS wear is permitted

If you have any questions as to what is or is not acceptable, please feel free to call the main office of the school.
